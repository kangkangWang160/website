How to Play

  • Choose a difficulty: Extra Easy, Normal, or Hard.
  • Click "Play Ping Pong" to start the game.
  • Move your paddle with your mouse or the left/right arrow keys.
  • Keep the ball in play by bouncing it off your paddle.
  • Try to score as many points as possible!
  • The game ends if the ball passes your paddle or the AI's paddle (except in Extra Easy mode).
