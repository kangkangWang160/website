How to Play

  • Choose a difficulty: Extra Easy, Normal, Hard, or Extra Hard.
  • Click "Play Ping Pong" to start the game.
  • Move your paddle with your mouse or the left/right arrow keys.
  • Keep the ball(s) in play by bouncing them off your paddle.
  • Try to score as many points as possible!
  • In Extra Hard mode, 3 balls are in play at once. Choose between Health Restore (gain health for saves) or No Restore (no health gain).
  • The game ends if health reaches 0 or all balls are lost.
